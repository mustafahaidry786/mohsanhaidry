About Me

Hi, I'm Mohsan Haidry

I am a Street Photographer who loves to capture every single movement. I'm not a Portrait photographer - my passion lies in capturing Historical Places, Travel Photography, and Candid Movements in Streets.

Originally from Nankana Sahib, the holy city of Sikh Religion, I've now moved to the vibrant city of Barcelona, Spain. Every street here is filled with history, making this city a heaven for history lovers like me.
